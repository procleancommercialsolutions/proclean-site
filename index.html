Our Services

Janitorial

Daily and weekly cleaning solutions to keep your business consistently spotless. We handle trash removal, surface cleaning, restrooms, and more.

Disinfecting

Advanced sanitization services using EPA-approved disinfectants to maintain a healthy, germ-free environment for your employees and customers.

Carpet Cleaning

Professional carpet care including routine vacuuming and periodic deep steam cleaning to prolong carpet life and appearance.

Deep Cleaning

Intensive floor-to-ceiling cleaning that leaves no corner untouched. Perfect for occasional deep clean projects or pre/post-event cleaning.

Hard Surface Floors

Specialized cleaning for tile, hardwood, concrete and more. We scrub, mop, buff, and polish floors to keep them shining and scuff-free.

High Dusting

Removal of dust and cobwebs from high ceilings, vents, and other hard-to-reach areas. We improve air quality by keeping those seldom-cleaned spaces dust-free.

Post-Construction

Comprehensive cleanup for newly built or renovated sites. We clear away construction dust and debris to make your space move-in ready.

Pressure Washing

High-pressure exterior cleaning for sidewalks, building exteriors, parking lots, and other surfaces, removing grime, graffiti, and stains.

Stripping & Waxing

Removal of old floor finish and application of new wax coating. We leave your hard floors with a protective, high-gloss finish that looks like new.

Upholstery

Gentle yet effective cleaning of office furniture, partitions, and upholstery. We remove stains and dirt while extending the life of your furnishings.

Window Cleaning

Interior and exterior window washing for a streak-free shine. We make sure your windows are crystal clear, enhancing your building's appearance.
