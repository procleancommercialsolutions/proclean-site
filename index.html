General

+ What is Commercial Cleaning?
Commercial cleaning involves professional janitorial services provided to businesses and institutions. It includes routine cleaning, sanitation, and maintenance tasks tailored to commercial properties.
+ Why is commercial cleaning important for your business?
Clean environments enhance employee productivity, reduce the spread of illness, and leave a positive impression on customers and clients.
+ Do you offer residential cleaning?
No, we specialize in commercial and industrial cleaning. However, we can recommend trusted partners who offer residential services.
+ How often do you clean?
We offer flexible cleaning schedules—daily, weekly, bi-weekly, or custom based on your facility’s needs.
+ What are the available cleaning schedule options?
You can choose from daily maintenance, weekly deep cleaning, or a hybrid schedule that fits your business operations.

Casinos

+ What are key cleaning areas in a casino?
Gaming floors, restrooms, slot machines, high-touch surfaces, and back-of-house areas require focused attention.
+ How should gaming floors be cleaned?
With low-noise, efficient equipment during off-hours to avoid disruption to players and machines.
+ How should casino restrooms be cleaned?
With continuous sanitation schedules and disinfectants that target odor and bacteria buildup.
+ What cleaning supplies are used in casinos?
Eco-friendly disinfectants, microfiber cloths, and vacuums with HEPA filters are commonly used.
